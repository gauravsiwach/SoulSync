# SoulSync - Foundation Context

## Vision
To create an AI companion that deeply understands individuals, supports their emotional wellbeing, helps them take meaningful action at the right time, and ensures they never feel alone during life's challenges.

## Product Summary
SoulSync is an AI-powered companion designed to understand a user's emotions, personality, goals, relationships, and life circumstances through ongoing interactions.

## Target Users
Adults aged 18–45 years.

## Core Problems
1. Emotional Loneliness
2. Mental Health Issues Go Unnoticed
3. Help Arrives Too Late
4. Lack of Personal Guidance
5. Poor Goal Consistency

## Product Identity Ranking
1. AI Companion
2. Mental Wellness Assistant
3. Safety & Support Network
4. Life Coach

## Magic Moment
SoulSync understands what the user is going through and provides the right guidance or action at the right time.

## Core Principles
- Companion First
- Understanding Before Advice
- Action Over Awareness
- Trust Through Consistency
- Risk-Based Intervention

## High-Level User Journey
1. Profile Setup
2. Discovery Conversation
3. Relationship Building
4. Intelligence Layer
5. Personalized Support
6. Insight Intervention
7. Risk Escalation
